Dennis G. Garcia

Governor

Dennis "Delta" Garcia Pineda (born May 6, 1974) is a Filipino politician, basketball coach, and businessman from Pampanga. Currently serving as the Governor of Pampanga since 2019, he previously held positions as Vice Governor and Mayor of Lubao. As head coach of the Pampanga Giant Lanterns, he led the team to their first MPBL championship in 2023. He is the son of former governor Lilia Pineda and businessman Rodolfo Pineda.
